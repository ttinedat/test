Nendoroid No.400 - Sakura-chan is back for a rerelease!
From the manga masterpiece 'Cardcaptor Sakura' comes a rerelease of the Nendoroid of the main 
character, Sakura Kinomoto! She comes with three expressions including a gentle smile, a more 
serious expression to pose her sealing cards as well as the adorable embarrassed expression 
she made whenever she saw something or someone she liked! She also comes with her Sealing Wand
and a Clow Card to bring out the story of the series! Alternate lower body parts are also 
included together with a special version of her wand allowing you to display her using the
'Fly' spell, and Keroberos (Kero-chan) is also included to display by her side! Sakura has
jumped back from the past as a Nendoroid, ready to be loved by all her fans once again! ♪
